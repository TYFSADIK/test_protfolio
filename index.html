Hello, I’m Taki.

This is my quiet corner of the web – a text-heavy log of homelab builds, networking, infosec experiments, and the occasional life note.

What this site is

A slow, minimalist archive. I write about building private infrastructure, self-hosting everything I can, learning security the hard way, and documenting the tools I actually use.

Expect: long-form posts, tiny notes, devlogs from my lab, curated links, and a small “grimoire” of infosec-only entries.
